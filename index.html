About Me

I'm Jolo Balbin, a software / AI engineer on a mission to make payments not suck. I'm currently leading an engineering team at Coda, a fintech company, building better payment experiences for people everywhere.

Before fintech took over my life, I was a Staff Engineer at Agoda, wrangling large-scale systems for millions of travelers. I'm also formerly the CTO of Indigo Research. My sweet spot is building software with AI capabilities and scaling them up into production without things blowing up.

I created TextTeaser, an open source automatic summarization algorithm for my thesis. Check it out at Github. I also have an experience in data science working with employment and telco data. I also have no problem in gathering datasets with my experience in scraping different stuff.

Outside of software, I do wall climbing and bouldering, join various sports activies, listening to audiobooks and living the life with people.
